### Describing One's Own Experience

描述自己的经历

In describing his own experience, the writer does not only tell what happened to himself, but also what the experience felt like, or exactly how he behaved. Often in a detailed description of what happened to him the writer finds himself adding the feelings he had during the experience, the thoughts he has as he relives the event. In retrospect, the writer puts things together in order, and adds his later understanding.

在描述自己的经历时，作者不仅讲述了发生在自己身上的事情，还描述了这种经历的感受，或者他当时具体的行为举止。通常，在详细描述发生在他身上的事情时，作者会发现自己不由自主地加入了他在经历过程中的感受，以及他在重温该事件时的想法。回顾过去，作者会将事物按顺序梳理，并加上他后来的理解。
