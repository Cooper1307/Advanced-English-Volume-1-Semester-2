> [!NOTE]
> 本文体裁为**自传体叙事散文**（autobiographical narrative essay），并非虚构小说。叙述者"I"即 Orwell 本人，基于其 1922–1927 年在缅甸任帝国警察的亲身经历写成。

**中文概括**：叙述者（Orwell）先以一句提纲挈领点明此事件让他看清了帝国主义的本质，随后详述事件经过——一头驯象发狂出逃，在市镇上毁屋、杀牛、洗劫果摊、掀翻垃圾车，叙述者接到电话后携一支对大象威力不足的老式 .44 温切斯特步枪前去处理。
**English Summary**: The narrator (Orwell) opens with a framing statement that this incident revealed to him the true nature of imperialism, then recounts the events: a tame elephant breaks free during a bout of "must" and wreaks havoc in the town — destroying a hut, killing a cow, raiding fruit-stalls, and overturning a rubbish van — prompting him, as a local police officer, to respond with only an old .44 Winchester rifle, far too weak to bring down an elephant.

---

**🔹 简要版**：一头驯象在镇上失控肆虐，身为警察的 Orwell 被迫带着根本打不死大象的步枪赶去处理——他日后将此事视为自己认清帝国主义本质的起点。
**🔹 In brief**: A tame elephant runs amok in town; Orwell, the local police officer, is called to deal with it armed only with a woefully inadequate rifle — an incident he later recognizes as the moment he saw through imperialism.
